# Compliance boundary

TuitionFlow is a technology and workflow demonstration. It is not an RBI-authorised payment
aggregator, authorised dealer bank, lender, tax collector, or money transmitter. The application
does not hold funds and all bundled partner integrations are deterministic simulations.

## Live-money gates

Real payments must remain disabled until the selected PA-CB/AD Category-I bank and Ripple approve
the fund flow, contracts, corridor, KYC/AML/CFT controls, FEMA/LRS reporting, purpose codes, tax,
refund handling, data residency, outsourcing controls, incident response, and reconciliation.

Render staging is restricted to synthetic identities and documents. A live deployment must use
partner-approved India-resident payment-data infrastructure where required, complete independent
security testing, and establish CERT-In and DPDP incident/grievance procedures.

The application records partner outcomes and evidence but does not make authoritative KYC, AML/CFT,
sanctions, FEMA/LRS, purpose-code, tax, refund, or suspicious-transaction decisions. Those remain
with the contracted PA-CB/AD Category-I bank. Payments Direct 2.0 is represented by a shaped adapter;
production submission is unavailable until Ripple supplies approved credentials and contracts.

Privacy notices are versioned in consent records. Access, correction, erasure, consent-withdrawal,
grievance, legal-hold, and retention workflows are implemented, but their production policies and
response periods must be approved by Indian counsel and the regulated partner.

The lender screen approves only disbursement from a previously sanctioned education loan. It does
not originate, underwrite, price, or sanction a loan.
